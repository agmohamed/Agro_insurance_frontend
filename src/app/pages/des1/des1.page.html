Indemnity based comprehensive
crop insurance

That crop insurance cover against total or partial crop loss due to

  • Flood
  • Drought
  • Excess water
  • Plant disease
  • Pest damage
  • Damage by wild animal
  • Other natural perils(storm effect, earthquake, natural disaster or arson)

The coverage starts on the date of planting until getting harvest of the crop

The insurance coverage and the premiums in crop insurance depend on the risk level

Claims wii be decided n field assessment by claim committee
